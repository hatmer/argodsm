Unittest that checks that pages are correctly prefetched.

This fence prevents both compiler and CPU reordering of stores (and loads) on a page level to ensure that tmp is contiguously mapped in the backing store. This is necessary to ensure a deterministic behaviour for the first-touch allocation policy.
